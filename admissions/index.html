Admissions

Application steps and round timelines.

A migrated admissions structure covering online application, review, interview, and enrollment confirmation.

Steps

01

Explore programs

Review degree, professional, and pathway options before selecting an application route.

02

Prepare materials

Collect transcripts, English proficiency evidence, recommendation letters, and optional portfolio materials.

03

Apply online

Submit the application through the CMKL portal and pay the non-refundable application fee.

04

Application review

Shortlisted candidates receive instructions for any next steps.

05

Interview if required

Some applicants join an interview and may discuss a project during self-introduction.

06

Confirm enrollment

Accepted students complete confirmation and pay the enrollment deposit credited toward first-semester tuition.

FAQ

How is English proficiency reviewed?

CMKL lists Duolingo 105+, TOEFL iBT 70+, or IELTS 6.0+ as recommended evidence, with waivers possible for English-medium schooling.

Can applicants submit optional materials?

Yes. Resume, portfolio, pitch deck, recommendation letters, and additional standardized results can strengthen an application.

How much is the seat deposit?

Upon acceptance, the enrollment deposit is 50,000 THB and is credited toward first-semester tuition.
